ABOUT

___Know about Samiksha Bhavsar___

BIRTH

Samiksha Bhavsar was born on 14 december 1999 at Ganj Basoda, Madhya pradesh , India. Her maternal family belongs in Bhopal.

EDUCATION

She studied at her home town from Bharat Mata Convent Senior Secondary School till her 10th grade after which she went into boarding school for 2 years at Macro Vision academy in Burhanpur. She is currently persuing her Btech. degree from IET-Davv, Indore

PERSON

Samiksha always prefers to do things indepently,alone as far as it is possible. apart from poetry she loves to do sketching and cooking occassionly . She always wishes to have free and sorted life.

POETRY

She always had keen interest in writing articles, stories and speehes(for other people) in her school time. In june, 2018 she started to write small 2-4 liners poetry on social media. She started recording her spoken poetry in august 2020.
